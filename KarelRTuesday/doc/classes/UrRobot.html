Public Class methods

new(street, avenue, direction, beepers, color = nil)

Place a robot at a given corner (street, avenue) facing a given direction, with an initial number of beepers in its beeper bag If color is nil its background will appear transparent, otherwise a robot will appear to carry a shield of the given color, say :red or :blue.
